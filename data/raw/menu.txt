Appetizers
Bruschetta: Toasted baguette slices topped with fresh tomatoes, garlic, basil, and a balsamic glaze.

Spinach Artichoke Dip: A creamy blend of spinach, artichokes, and cheese, served hot with tortilla chips.

Calamari: Crispy fried calamari with a side of marinara sauce.

Main Courses
Gemini Burger: A classic beef patty with lettuce, tomato, onion, and your choice of cheese on a brioche bun. Served with fries.

Veggie Burger: A delicious plant-based patty with avocado, sprouts, and a vegan aioli on a whole-wheat bun. Served with a side salad.

Grilled Salmon: Perfectly grilled salmon fillet with a lemon-dill sauce, served with roasted asparagus and quinoa.

Chicken Parmesan: Breaded chicken breast topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese, served with spaghetti.

Desserts
Chocolate Lava Cake: A rich, warm chocolate cake with a molten center, served with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream.

Cheesecake: New York style cheesecake with a strawberry drizzle.

Drinks
Iced Tea

Soda (Coke, Diet Coke, Sprite)

Fresh Squeezed Lemonade

Ordering Information
How to Order: To place an order, please state the item name and quantity. For example, "I would like one Gemini Burger and an Iced Tea."

Confirmation: A team member will confirm your order and provide an estimated wait time.

Payments: We accept all major credit cards.

Special Requests: For any allergies or dietary restrictions, please mention them when you place your order.
